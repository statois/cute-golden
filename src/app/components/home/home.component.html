    About Us

    Our Golden Retriever House help responsible Golden Retriever breeders place their puppies with caring individuals and families across the U.S. But, more than just a service, We are is a community of Dog lovers whose mission is to make lives better by placing healthy puppies/dogs into happy homes. The Golden Retriever House brand with puppies at the center, is rooted in the belief that pets in general should be celebrated, and owners deserve to experience trust, confidence and transparency when searching for their new companions.
